BAIUST Proposed Permanent Campus

The main campus of BAIUST is situated in Comilla, supported by the infrastructure of Cumilla Cantonment. Comilla, a vibrant city in eastern Bangladesh within the Chittagong Division, provides an ideal environment for learning and growth. The university spans 14 acres, offering modern facilities for students and faculty.
