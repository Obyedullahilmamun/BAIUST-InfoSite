About


Bangladesh Army International University of Science and Technology (BAIUST), affiliated with the Bangladesh Army, started its journey on 14 February 2015.


Undergraduate Programs

  • Faculty of Electrical and Computer Engineering
    1. Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering
    2. Department of Computer Science and Engineering
    3. Department of Information and Communication Technology

  • Faculty of Civil Engineering
    1. Department of Civil Engineering

  • Faculty of Business
    1. Department of Business Administration
    2. Department of Economics

  • Faculty of Science & Humanities
    1. Department of English
    2. Department of Law
    3. Department of Science and Humanities

BAIUST aims to provide quality education guided by its motto — “Knowledge, Wisdom and Technology.” It also encourages creativity and innovation so that students are ready for the future. As BAIUST grows, new departments and academic opportunities will be introduced to enrich its educational offerings and contribute to national development.
